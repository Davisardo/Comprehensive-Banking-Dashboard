## Data Used  
- **Data** – Financial and customer dataset containing key metrics such as total customers, total balance, and demographic details.  
- **Data Preparation** – Microsoft Excel  
- **Data Modeling & Measures (DAX)** – Power BI  
- **Data Visualization** – Power BI  

## Objectives  
1. Analyze customer distribution by state, gender, and age group.  
2. Track the total number of customers and balance over time.  
3. Measure key financial metrics such as total balance and average customer age.  
4. Provide regional insights through an interactive map.  
5. Design a clean, user-friendly dashboard with dynamic navigation and KPIs.  

## Summary of Findings  
- The dashboard includes **5,000 total customers** with an average age of **43.47 years**.  
- The **total balance** across all customers amounts to **$25,302,855**.  
- States such as **Texas, California, and Florida** have the highest customer concentration.  
- Customer distribution by gender is fairly balanced between male and female.  
- The **average balance** across all card types (MasterCard, Visa, and AMEX) is nearly equal, showing consistent usage patterns.  
- The largest customer group is aged **35–44**, followed by **26–35**, indicating strong engagement from mid-age customers.  
- The dashboard enables users to explore performance metrics by region, card type, and customer segment interactively.  

## Key Features  
- Modern, clean, and professional **UI design**.  
- **Interactive KPI cards** for quick insights into major metrics.  
- **Dynamic visuals** including maps, bar charts, and donut charts.  
- **Clickable sidebar navigation** (Demographics, Transaction, Loan & Credit, Customer).  
- Fully interactive dashboard designed for financial and customer analytics.  

## Outcomes  
**This dashboard helps businesses to:**  
- Understand customer demographics and regional trends.  
- Monitor key financial KPIs in real time.  
- Identify top-performing customer segments and regions.  
- Enhance decision-making through clear, data-driven insights.
